<span data-ttu-id="7a676-105">本文介绍 Microsoft HoloLens 体验团队在创建 [RoboRaid](https://www.microsoft.com/p/roboraid/9nblggh5fv3j)的音频时遇到的独特挑战，这是一种混合现实的第一员射击。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-105">This article describes the unique challenges that the Microsoft HoloLens Experience Team encountered when creating audio for [RoboRaid](https://www.microsoft.com/p/roboraid/9nblggh5fv3j), a mixed-reality first-person shooter.</span></span>

## <a name="the-tech"></a><span data-ttu-id="7a676-106">技术人员</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-106">The tech</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-107">[空间音效](spatial-sound.md) 是 Microsoft HoloLens 最令人兴奋的一项功能，它为用户提供了一种方法，让用户了解当对象不见视线时，会发生什么情况。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-107">[Spatial sound](spatial-sound.md) is one of the most exciting features of Microsoft HoloLens, providing a way for users to perceive what's going on around them when objects are out of the line of sight.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-108">在 RoboRaid 中，空间音质最明显且最有效的使用情况是提醒播放机在用户的外围视力之外发生的事情。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-108">In RoboRaid, the most obvious and effective use of spatial sound is to alert the player to something happening outside of the user's peripheral vision.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-109">例如，Breacher 可以输入房间中的任何扫描壁，但如果不是面对其进入位置，可能会错过。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-109">For example, the Breacher can enter from any of the scanned walls in the room, but if you're not facing the location where it's entering, you might miss it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-110">为了提醒您此 invasion，您将收到 Breacher 进入的位置的不同位音频，这让您知道您需要快速地停止它。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-110">To alert you to this invasion, you'll hear a distinct bit of audio coming from where the Breacher is entering, which lets you know that you need to act quickly to stop it.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-115">在 CPU 上可以要求空间音质。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-115">Spatial sound can be demanding on the CPU.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-116">对于繁忙的体验（如 RoboRaid），在任何给定时间都需要将空间音质的实例保留为小于8。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-116">For a busy experience like RoboRaid it was crucial to keep the instances of spatial sound to under eight at any given time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-117">在大多数情况下，只需设置不同音频事件的实例限制，就可以轻松地终止在达到限制后发生的任何实例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-117">For the most part, it was as easy as setting the limit of instances of different audio events so that any instances that happen after the limit is reached are killed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-118">例如，在无人机生成时，在任何给定时间，其 screams 限制为三个实例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-118">For example, when drones spawn, their screams are limited to three instances at any given time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-119">如果只考虑四个无人机可以一次生成，三个 screams 都非常多，因为您的大脑无法跟踪很多发音相似的音频事件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-119">Considering only about four drones can spawn at once, three screams are plenty since there’s no way your brain can keep track of that many similar-sounding audio events.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-120">这会释放其他空间声音事件的资源，例如敌人爆炸或敌人准备进行拍摄。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-120">This freed up resources for other spatial sound events, like enemy explosions or enemies preparing to shoot.</span></span>

### <a name="communicating-a-hit"></a><span data-ttu-id="7a676-139">通信命中</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-139">Communicating a hit</span></span>

### <a name="getting-big-sound-from-small-speakers"></a><span data-ttu-id="7a676-149">从小扬声器获得大声音</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-149">Getting big sound from small speakers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-150">HoloLens 扬声器很小，并且非常适合于设备的需求，因此您无法听到太多的低端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-150">HoloLens speakers are small and light to suit the needs of the device, so you can’t expect to hear too much low-end.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-151">与开发智能手机或手持游戏设备类似，声音设计人员和作曲者必须注意其音频的频率内容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-151">Similar to developing for smart phones or handheld gaming devices, sound designers and composers have to be mindful of the frequency content of their audio.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-152">我始终设计声音或使用全频率范围写入音乐，因为戴耳机是用户的一个选项。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-152">I always design sounds or write music with full frequency range because wearing headphones is an option for the users.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-153">但是，为了确保与 HoloLens 扬声器的兼容性，我偶尔运行测试，方法是在发生的任何 DAW 的主节点中放置一个 EQ。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-153">However, to ensure compatibility with HoloLens speakers, I run a test occasionally by putting an EQ in the master of any DAW I happen to be working in.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-154">EQ 设置由围绕600到 700 Hz 的高传递筛选器， (不是太陡) 并且低 pass 筛选器大约为 10K (非常陡) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-154">The EQ setting consists of a high-pass filter around 600 to 700 Hz (not too steep) and low-pass filter at around 10K (very steep).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-155">这会让你大概如何在设备上播放声音。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-155">That should give you a ballpark idea of how your sounds will playback on the device.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-156">如果你依赖于低音在音乐中进行更改，则可能会发现，当你应用此 EQ 设置时，音乐完全丢失了 root。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-156">If you are relying on bass to give the sense of chord changing in your music, you may find that your music completely loses the sense of root when you apply this EQ setting.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-157">若要解决此情况，我向低音添加了另一层，这是一个 octave 更高的 (，具有一些丰富的谐波) ，并将其混合在一起以获得 root。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-157">To remedy this, I added another layer to the bass that is one octave higher (with some rich harmonics) and mixed it to get the sense of root back.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-158">有时，使用扭曲来使谐波会在上部范围内提供足够的频率内容，以使大脑认为它下面有一些内容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-158">Sometimes using distortion to amp up the harmonics will give enough frequency content in the upper range to make our brain think that there’s something underneath it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-159">这种情况适用于类似于影响、爆炸或声音等一段时间的，例如老板的超级攻击。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-159">This is true for SFX like impacts, explosions, or sounds for special moments, such as a boss' super attacks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-160">你确实不能依赖低端来使玩家了解影响或权重。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-160">You really can’t rely on the low-end to give the player a sense of impact or weight.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-161">与音乐一样，使用扭曲来提供少量捕获。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-161">As with music, using distortion to give a little bit of crunch definitely helped.</span></span>

### <a name="making-your-audio-cues-stand-out"></a><span data-ttu-id="7a676-162">使您的音频提示突出</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-162">Making your audio cues stand out</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-163">当然，团队中的每个人都希望 bombastic 音乐、枪支和古怪爆炸;但他们还希望能够听到 voiceover 或任何其他游戏关键的音频提示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-163">Naturally, everyone on the team wanted bombastic music, loud guns, and crazy explosions; but they also wanted to be able to hear voiceover or any other game-critical audio cues.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7a676-164">在具有各种频率的控制台游戏上，你可以根据声音的重要性，使用更多的选项来划分频率。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-164">On a console game with full range of frequency you have more options to divide frequencies up depending on the importance of the sound.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-165">但对于 RoboRaid，我限制了从声音弯曲的频率范围。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-165">For RoboRaid, however, I was limited in the number of ranges of frequencies I could curve out from sounds.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a676-166">例如，如果使用低刀筛选器并使其从较高的范围内弯曲，则不会有任何内容出现在声音上，因为没有太多的低端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a676-166">For instance, if you use low-pass filter and curve out too much from the higher end of the spectrum, you won’t have anything left on the sound because there’s not much low-end.</span></span>
