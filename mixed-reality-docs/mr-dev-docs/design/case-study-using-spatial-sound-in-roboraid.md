## <a name="the-tech"></a><span data-ttu-id="2898a-106">技术人员</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-106">The tech</span></span>

### <a name="getting-big-sound-from-small-speakers"></a><span data-ttu-id="2898a-152">从小扬声器获得大声音</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-152">Getting big sound from small speakers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2898a-153">HoloLens 扬声器很小，并且非常适合于设备的需求，因此您无法听到太多的低端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-153">HoloLens speakers are small and light to suit the needs of the device, so you can’t expect to hear too much low-end.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2898a-154">与开发智能手机或手持游戏设备类似，声音设计人员和作曲者必须注意其音频的频率内容。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-154">Similar to developing for smart phones or handheld gaming devices, sound designers and composers have to be mindful of the frequency content of their audio.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2898a-155">我始终设计声音或使用全频率范围写入音乐，因为戴耳机是用户的一个选项。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-155">I always design sounds or write music with full frequency range because wearing headphones is an option for the users.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2898a-156">但是，为了确保与 HoloLens 扬声器的兼容性，我偶尔运行测试，方法是在发生的任何 DAW 的主节点中放置一个 EQ。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-156">However, to ensure compatibility with HoloLens speakers, I run a test occasionally by putting an EQ in the master of any DAW I happen to be working in.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2898a-157">EQ 设置由围绕 600 Hz 的高通滤波器到 700 Hz， (不是太陡) 和低刀筛选器大约为 10K (陡) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-157">The EQ setting consists of a high-pass filter around 600 Hz to 700 Hz (not too steep) and low-pass filter at around 10K (steep).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2898a-158">这应该会使您大致了解一下声音如何播放到设备上。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2898a-158">That should give you an approximate idea of how your sounds will play back on the device.</span></span>
